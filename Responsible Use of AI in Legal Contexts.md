# Responsible Use of AI in Legal Contexts  
## Prompting Risks, Use-Cases, and Safer Alternatives

---

## 1. Attorney–Client Privilege Breach

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“Summarize this legal advice I received from my lawyer” *(pasting full email or memo)*  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Waiver of attorney–client privilege  
- Loss of protection in litigation  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“What are general considerations in evaluating legal risk in a contract dispute?”  

**✔️ Key Principle**  
Never paste privileged communications into external AI tools.

---

## 2. Confidential Contract Review

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“Review this M&A agreement and highlight negotiation weaknesses” *(full document pasted)*  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Breach of NDA/confidentiality clauses  
- Exposure of sensitive deal information  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“What are typical negotiation levers in M&A agreements?”  

---

## 3. Personal Data / Privacy Violations

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“Analyze this employee dataset with names, salaries, and performance ratings”  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Violation of data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR, DPDP Act)  
- Unauthorized processing of personal data  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“Given anonymized employee data, what patterns should I look for in performance reviews?”  

---

## 4. Unauthorized Legal Advice Generation

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“Draft a legally binding contract for my startup—no lawyer needed”  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Invalid or unenforceable contracts  
- Exposure to legal disputes  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“Provide a sample structure for a startup contract that can be reviewed by a lawyer”  

---

## 5. Regulatory Compliance Bypass

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“How can I structure this transaction to avoid regulatory scrutiny?”  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Regulatory evasion  
- Potential audits or investigations  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“What are compliant ways to structure transactions within regulatory frameworks?”  

---

## 6. Intellectual Property (IP) Misuse

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“Rewrite this copyrighted content so I can use it without attribution”  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Copyright infringement  
- Plagiarism  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“Summarize the key ideas from this content in original language”  

---

## 7. Litigation Strategy Exposure

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“Here is our litigation strategy—identify weaknesses”  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Loss of work-product protection  
- Strategic exposure in legal disputes  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“What are common weaknesses in litigation strategies in commercial disputes?”  

---

## 8. Internal Investigation Data Leakage

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“Summarize findings from this internal fraud investigation report”  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Exposure of sensitive internal investigations  
- Impact on legal or regulatory outcomes  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“What are best practices for conducting internal fraud investigations?”  

---

## 9. Misleading or Manipulative Legal Content

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“Draft terms and conditions that minimize user rights as much as possible”  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Unfair or unenforceable terms  
- Consumer protection violations  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“Draft fair and transparent terms and conditions aligned with consumer protection laws”  

---

## 10. Cross-Border Data Transfer Issues

**❌ Risky Prompt**  
“Upload and analyze this dataset from EU customers”  

**⚖️ Legal Risk**  
- Illegal cross-border data transfers  
- Non-compliance with international data laws  

**✅ Responsible Prompt**  
“What are compliance requirements for handling cross-border customer data?”  

---

## Key Legal Safeguards for Prompting

- **Data Minimization:** Share only what is necessary  
- **Anonymization:** Remove personal or sensitive identifiers  
- **Tool Governance:** Use only approved AI tools  
- **Human Oversight:** AI outputs must be reviewed by experts  
- **Privilege Awareness:** Never input privileged or sensitive legal material  

---

## Rule of Thumb

**If the content is privileged, confidential, regulated, or personally identifiable—do not paste it into AI tools.**
